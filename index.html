Trip Teasers is a quick way to share information between people about little known places or far-off locations. Well-seasoned travel agents have been a major source of information for those who like to experience life in spots both near and far. With Trip Teazers a new avenue is opened to gain this knowledge.

Trip Teasers has been a great way to plan my trip to Italy. I have been able to sort out where I should go first, how long it will take, how much money I need, what I will see, and why some towns are more exciting to me than others. Without Trip Teazers, this could have really taken a long time. It has seemed short because the process is a lot of FUN.
